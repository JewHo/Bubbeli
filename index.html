Bubbeli:

Sebastian "Bubbeli" Lindgren is a 24-year-old indie artist from Turku, Finland. Bubbeli's music is a mix of indie-pop, reggae and funky vibes. He writes mainly in English, but also in his first language Swedish as well as in Finnish.

The Runeberg Orchestra:

A collective of musicians and friends backing up Bubbeli on live shows. The band features a small horn section, keyboards, guitar and bass.
